Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

VHabits collects minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information (username, email, password)
  • Habit data you create and track
  • Usage statistics for app improvement

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the VHabits service
  • Sync your habits across devices
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Send important service notifications

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure password storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

Third-Party Services

VHabits does not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your habit data

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our GitHub repository.
